\chapter*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}

This Master's Thesis analyzes the security of OTT streaming ecosystems protected with Widevine DRM. Its main goal is to study which guarantees Widevine provides, which threats remain outside its protection model, and how a defense-in-depth architecture can complement DRM in real-world content distribution scenarios.
